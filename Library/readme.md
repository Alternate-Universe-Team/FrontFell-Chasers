# Meet the Characters

Welcome to the world of Storm and his friends! Here you will find details about the main characters of this universe.

---

## About Storm
![Storm](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Stormwindsky/StormWorld/refs/heads/main/Library/Characters/Stormwindsky/Storm2.svg)

* **Age:** 16
* **Gender:** Male
* **Role:** Main Protagonist

**Personality:**
Storm is a brave and energetic teenager. Always ready for an adventure, he is known for his loyalty to his friends and his protective nature. Despite being only 16, he shows great leadership skills, though he can sometimes be a bit impulsive when his loved ones are in trouble.

---

## About CloudySunny
![CloudySunny](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Stormwindsky/StormWorld/refs/heads/main/Library/Characters/CloudySunny/CloudySunny2.svg)

* **Age:** 16
* **Gender:** Female
* **Relationship:** Storm's Best Friend

**Personality:**
CloudySunny is a bright and cheerful girl who acts as the "light" of the group. As Storm's best friend, she provides the perfect balance to his energy with her calm and thoughtful approach to problems. She is highly empathetic and always knows how to cheer someone up when they are feeling down.

---

## About Orango
![Orango](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Stormwindsky/StormWorld/refs/heads/main/Library/Characters/Orango/Orango.svg)

* **Age:** 6
* **Gender:** Male
* **Role:** Storm's Pet

**Personality:**
Orango is Storm's unique and loyal companion. Don't let his age of 6 fool you; he is incredibly smart for a pet and often understands situations better than humans do. He is playful, loves orange snacks, and is never far from Storm's side. He has a curious nature and a very friendly disposition.

---

## About Robert
![Orango](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Stormwindsky/StormWorld/refs/heads/main/Library/Characters/Robert/Robert2.svg)

* **Age:** 17
* **Gender:** Male
* **Relationship:** Storm's Childhood Best Friend

**Personality:**
Robert is the grounded, technical heart of the group. Having grown up alongside Storm, he is the one who truly understands his friend's restless energy and is always there to balance it out. While Storm is quick to jump into action, Robert is the one who makes sure they have a plan—and the gear—to survive it. He is a bit of a "gadget guy" with a dry sense of humor that often surfaces when things get chaotic. He is deeply loyal, and because of their lifelong bond, he never hesitates to follow Storm into the unknown, no matter how cautious he might personally be.
